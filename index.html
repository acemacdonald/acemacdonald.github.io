About Me

I have a formal education in economics, receiving an MComm Economics from Stellenbosch University in 2018. Thereafter I spent almost five years at one of South Africa's top consulting firm, specialising in Financial Engineering while working on large strategic change projects. I have recently completed my MBA studies from the Said Business School at University of Oxford where I was proviledged enough to immerse myself in advanced subject matter ranging from Strategy and Finance, to Negotiations and Impact Investing. I have a passion for nature and sustainability, spending almost every weekend growing up at our family farm in the Cederberg. My love for natural spaces has heavily influenced who I am today, which at my core is a person who seeks to preserve our natural world for generations to come.
